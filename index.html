About Me

AJ Music - Asif Jah

"جہاں طب کا علم دل کو شفا دیتا ہے، وہاں موسیقی روح کو سکون بخشتی ہے"
"Where medical knowledge heals the heart, music brings peace to the soul"

By profession, I am a Doctor - dedicated to healing and serving humanity. Yet, my heart has always belonged to the mystical world of music. From the tender age of childhood, the melodies of Sufi kalam, the soul-stirring verses of ghazals, the profound beauty of shayari, and the vibrant rhythms of Bollywood have been my constant companions.

Music flows through my veins not as a profession, but as a sacred hobby - a divine calling that transcends the boundaries of my medical practice. In the quiet moments between healing bodies, I find myself lost in the raags and taals that have shaped my very being since childhood.

طبیب بھی، فنکار بھی (Healer & Artist)

A unique journey where the science of medicine meets the art of music. By day, I serve as a healer, bringing comfort to those in need. By evening, I immerse myself in the mystical world of Sufi music, where every note becomes a prayer and every melody a meditation.

بچپن سے عشق موسیقی (Love Since Childhood)

From childhood, my heart resonated with the divine verses of Amir Khusrau, the soulful ghazals of Ghalib, and the timeless melodies of Bollywood's golden era. This passion, nurtured over decades, has blossomed into AJ Music - where healing hands create healing melodies.

"موسیقی دل کی زبان ہے، جو الفاظ کہہ نہیں سکتے، وہ سُر کہہ دیتے ہیں"

"Music is the language of the heart; what words cannot express, melodies convey"
